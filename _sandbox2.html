Design Principles

1. No single-outline hulls. Every ship built from 3+ distinct connected sections.
2. Bridges are always tapered or angular, never square. They imply a forward-facing viewport area.
3. Cargo modules use warm yellow stroke. Hull/structure uses cyan. Weapons use red. Mining uses purple.
4. Connectors (spines, pylons) link sections visibly - ships look constructed, not grown.
5. Internal detail lines show paneling/segmentation within modules (implies depth).
6. Every component should suggest a 3D form that could be extrapolated to a side/3-4 view.
7. Larger ships = more modules. Tier scales by adding sections, not stretching one shape.
