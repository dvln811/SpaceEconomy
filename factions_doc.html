Factions & Corporations

The galaxy is divided among six major factions, each controlling sectors of space and pursuing their own agendas. Within each faction, corporations handle specific operations. NPC ships belong to these corps, and in the future, players will be able to join or create factions.

Future: Player Factions
Players will eventually be able to create their own factions, recruit NPC corps, and compete for territory. Think Mount & Blade II: NPCs with their own goals can be convinced to join your cause through reputation, money, or aligned interests.
Terran Federation (TFD)
"Order through unity. Central governance, regulated markets, strong navy."

The largest and most established faction. Controls core space with an iron fist wrapped in bureaucracy. Safe, stable, but restrictive. High taxes, strict trade regulations, but excellent infrastructure and protection.

Territory: CoreAllies: Nexus CollectiveEnemies: Frontier Alliance, Corsairs
Federal Navy Military
Armed forces of the Federation. Patrols core space, enforces law, operates gate checkpoints.
StellarCorp Industries Manufacturing
Largest manufacturer. Hull plating, engines, station modules. Government contracts.
Aegis Defense Systems Military
Premier weapons and shield manufacturer. Military-only contracts, restricted sales.
Federal Transit Authority Trade
Operates jump gates, regulates commerce, collects transit fees.
Nexus Collective (NXC)
"Knowledge is power. Research, innovation, technological supremacy."

A technocracy driven by scientific advancement. Controls key research installations and produces the most advanced technology. Secretive, elitist, but their tech is the best in the sector.

Territory: CoreAllies: Terran FederationEnemies: Corsairs
Nexus Research Labs Research
Cutting-edge R&D. Quantum filaments, exotic materials, prototype tech.
Helix Biomedical Research
Pharmaceuticals, nanite paste, life support, cloning technology.
QuantumCore Ltd Manufacturing
Superconductors, processors, AI cores, drone brains.
Deep Space Cartography Research
System surveys, anomaly research, navigation data, exploration.
Merchants Guild (MGD)
"Profit is progress. Free markets, minimal regulation, trade above all."

A loose confederation of trading houses and corporations. They don't conquer territory, they buy it. Controls the rim's trade hubs and sets commodity prices across the sector.

Territory: RimAllies: Frontier AllianceEnemies: Terran Federation
Galactic Exchange Trade
Operates the largest commodity markets. Sets baseline prices, market data.
Voidway Logistics Trade
Bulk hauling, supply chain management, courier services across the sector.
Aurelia Banking Consortium Trade
Financial services, insurance, contracts, escrow, loans.
Smelter's Union Mining
Refinery operations across rim space. Bulk ore processing, fuel production.
Frontier Alliance (FRA)
"Freedom above all. Self-governance, personal liberty, no central authority."

A coalition of independent colonies, miners, and settlers who reject Federation control. Decentralized, scrappy, resourceful. They help each other but answer to no one.

Territory: RimAllies: Merchants GuildEnemies: Terran Federation
Rockbreaker Collective Mining
Independent mining cooperatives. Deep core specialists, rare ore extraction.
Frontier Militia Military
Volunteer defense force. Anti-pirate patrols, colony protection.
Wraith Salvage Mining
Wreck recovery, decommissioning, scrap recycling, battlefield cleanup.
Terraform Pioneers Trade
Colony establishment, bio-dome farming, frontier supply runs.
Iron Compact (IRC)
"Strength through industry. Military-industrial complex, expansion, conquest."

An authoritarian military state built on heavy industry. Expansionist, aggressive, well-organized. They build warships and use them. Currently consolidating frontier territory.

Territory: FrontierAllies: NoneEnemies: Nexus Collective, Frontier Alliance
Crucible Forge Works Manufacturing
Heavy weapons, capital ship components, orbital weapon platforms.
Compact Armada Military
The war machine. Battlecruisers, carriers, conquest fleets.
Deepvein Extraction Mining
Strip mining operations. Aggressive, no-holds-barred resource acquisition.
Shadow Bureau Military
Intelligence, espionage, sabotage, black operations.
The Corsairs (CRS)
"Take what you can. Piracy, smuggling, black markets, no rules."

Not a faction in the traditional sense. A loose network of pirates, smugglers, and criminals operating in lawless space. They prey on traders, fence stolen goods, and sell illegal modifications.

Territory: Frontier (Null-sec)Allies: NoneEnemies: Terran Federation, Nexus Collective, Merchants Guild
Blood Fangs Military
Pirate fleet. Ambushes, gate camps, hostage ransoms, territory raids.
Black Bazaar Trade
Contraband markets, stolen goods fencing, illegal modifications.
Shadow Runners Trade
Smuggling networks. Moves banned goods through blockades and checkpoints.
Scrapyard Genius Manufacturing
Jury-rigged weapons, illegal mods, combat boosters, hacked drones.
