T4-T5: Products & Equipment

Components (T4) are assembled at Component Factories from T3 manufactured materials. Final products (T5) are built at Shipyards and are the end-use items: modules that can be fitted to ships, traded, or consumed.

Design Notes

Consumption & Demand Sinks:
- Ammunition is consumed (creates constant demand for T3 materials)
- Drones can be destroyed (need replacing)
- Ships can be destroyed (all fitted modules lost)
- Nanite paste consumed by repair modules
- Fuel consumed by travel

Price Tiers:
- T4 Components: 2,000 - 15,000 credits
- T5 Standard Equipment: 8,000 - 50,000 credits
- T5 Advanced/Exotic: 50,000 - 80,000 credits

Volume Design: Fitted modules have volume (matters for transport/market). Ammo is relatively bulky to make logistics meaningful. Drones are 5 m3 each (limits how many you can carry).

Future: Tech Levels
Each product can have Mk.I/Mk.II/Mk.III variants that require progressively rarer inputs (Mk.II needs rare crystals, Mk.III needs exotics). Same slot, better stats, much more expensive.
